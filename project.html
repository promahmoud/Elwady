About El Wady Import & Export

El Wady Import & Export is an Egyptian company specialized in cultivating, producing, and exporting a wide range of natural herbs, spices, and seeds. Located in Beni Suef – Samsta Center, we proudly bring the richness of Egypt’s fertile lands to markets around the world.

With a strong commitment to quality and sustainability, our mission is to deliver fresh, organic, and carefully handpicked products that meet the highest international standards and exceed our partners’ expectations.
